About Us

8 BitWarriors is a vibrant community of tech enthusiasts, programmers who are dedicated to fostering innovation, creativity, and technical prowess among students. The community provides a platform for members to explore and develop skills in various fields of technology. Through workshops, hackathons, internships, mentorships, guest lectures, tech talks and collaborative projects, the community aims to enhance practical knowledge and problem-solving abilities. It also encourages participation in coding competitions, promoting a culture of learning and excellence. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced tech enthusiast, 8bitWarriors offers ample opportunities to grow and make a meaningful impact. The community consists of self-motivated students and experts who gather together to come up with innovative ideas and work on them. It aims to technically strengthen the students by integrating their skills in the various fields of Engineering & Technology, so as to cope up with the highly competitive environment.
